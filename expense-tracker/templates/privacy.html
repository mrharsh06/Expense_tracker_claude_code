Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us. This includes your email address and any expenses you log, along with their associated categories, amounts, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect or store sensitive financial information such as credit card numbers or bank account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. Your expense records are used to generate summaries, visualizations, and insights about your spending patterns. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, or other operational purposes. These services may have access to your data only as necessary to perform their functions. We require all third parties to adhere to confidentiality and data protection obligations consistent with this policy.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also export your expense records in a portable format. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@spendly.example. We are committed to addressing your concerns in a timely and transparent manner.
